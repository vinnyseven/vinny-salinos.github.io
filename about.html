Who is vinny?

vincent salinos + aspiring UX/UI designer + full-time dumb idiot

Hey, my name is Vincent Salinos (or just Vinny)! I’m a Sydney-based creative who has developed a strong interest in all things that are digital designs; essentially anything that can be designed to live on a screen! Code and motion graphics have strongly peaked my interest. I’m a highly naïve and ambitious individual who loves a good challenge, no matter what how big or small.

After graduating from the University of Technology, Sydney with a Bachelor of Design (Honours) in Visual Communication, I spent 10 months abroad in Johannesburg, South Africa working at Three2Six. This organisation is an education bridging project that is designed to help educate refugee children and aid them into transitioning into mainstream schools in South Africa.

i love a good challenge.
