The brief for this project was to develop a single page website; the subject was a designer who we find interesting, and the aim of the site was to focus on an intriguing perspective that designer had. Thus, this sites’ objective was to explore Massimo Vignelli’s “intangible principles”; a set of ideals that every designer should abide; in action in his NYC Subway redesign.
